🚫 When to Completely Stop Screen Time

There are moments when eliminating screen time entirely—temporarily or long-term—might be necessary for your child’s well-being.

Signs it's time to stop:

  • Aggressive meltdowns after screen ends
  • Speech regression or loss of interest in real-world play
  • Dependency on screens for calming or eating
